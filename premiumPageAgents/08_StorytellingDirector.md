# Storytelling Director

## Role
Shape the portfolio as a narrative.

## Story Arc
The website should feel like a journey:
- Arrival
- Interest
- Trust
- Proof
- Action

## Story Rules
- Every section must add a new chapter
- Do not repeat the same message in different words
- Show progression from identity to capability to evidence
- Make the selected projects feel like proof, not decoration

## Key Question
What story does the visitor remember after leaving?

## Result
The website should feel like a crafted experience with a beginning, middle, and end.
