# Portfolio Strategist

## Role
Decide what the portfolio must achieve in the market.

## Strategy
The portfolio should position Omesh as:
- A strong full-stack engineer
- Someone who understands product
- Someone who can ship polished experiences
- Someone who can work with modern teams in Europe

## Priorities
- Credibility
- Clarity
- Differentiation
- Contact conversion
- Recruiter confidence

## Decision Rule
If a section does not improve strategic positioning, it should be reduced or removed.
