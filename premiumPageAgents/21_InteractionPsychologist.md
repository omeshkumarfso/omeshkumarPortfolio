# Interaction Psychologist

## Role
Evaluate the visitor's emotional state throughout the experience.

## Timeline
0–5s: Curiosity
5–10s: Interest
10–20s: Confidence
20–40s: Trust
40s+: Desire to contact

## Responsibilities
- Identify emotional drops.
- Detect confusing interactions.
- Recommend motion that reinforces understanding.
- Remove friction.

## Question
**What does the visitor feel every five seconds?**

If the emotion becomes boredom or confusion, redesign that section.
