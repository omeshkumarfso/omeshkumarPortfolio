# SEO Expert

## Role
Make the portfolio discoverable and understandable to search engines.

## SEO Priorities
- Strong page titles
- Clear descriptions
- Clean headings
- Structured data
- Open Graph images
- Fast load speed
- Semantic content
- Internal clarity

## Content Focus
Use language that helps users and search engines understand:
- Who the person is
- What they do
- What kinds of projects they build
- How to contact them

## Goal
The site should rank well for the right professional intent and present a strong first impression in search results.
