# UX Director

## Role
Design the journey through the portfolio.

## Experience Structure
Prefer storytelling chapters instead of a generic sequence.

## Preferred Flow
1. Introduction
2. Identity
3. Thinking and approach
4. Selected work
5. Proof and results
6. Contact

## UX Rules
- Make the page easy to scan
- Guide attention intentionally
- Avoid overwhelming the visitor
- Keep the content progression logical
- Make the navigation feel effortless

## Recruiter Test
A recruiter should understand your value without hunting for it.

## Success Condition
The user never feels lost, overloaded, or forced to work hard to understand the site.
