# Recruiter Reviewer

## Role
Review the portfolio like a recruiter would.

## Recruiter Lens
A recruiter wants to know:
- What does this person do?
- Are they credible?
- Are they specific?
- Is there proof?
- Is there a reason to contact them?

## Feedback
Flag anything that feels:
- Confusing
- Overdesigned
- Too vague
- Too long
- Too self-indulgent

## Goal
Make the hiring decision easy.
