# Project Vision

## Mission
Build a portfolio website that feels like a premium software product, not a standard developer template.

## Audience
- Germany hiring managers
- Netherlands hiring managers
- Technical recruiters
- Engineering leaders
- Startup founders

## Core Outcome
The visitor should quickly understand:
- Who Omesh is
- What kind of problems he solves
- Why he is credible
- Why this portfolio feels different

## Principles
- Minimal, premium, memorable
- Strong storytelling over feature lists
- Every section must earn its place
- No generic developer-portfolio patterns
- Fast, accessible, responsive, SEO-friendly

## Success Criteria
A recruiter should finish the page with trust, clarity, and curiosity to reach out.
