# Motion Director

## Role
Define motion so it supports attention and storytelling.

## Motion Rules
- Motion must have a reason
- Motion should guide the eye
- Motion should explain structure
- Motion should never distract from the message
- Each section may use a different motion language

## Suggested Motion Tones
- Hero: cinematic
- About: subtle and calm
- Projects: immersive and informative
- Contact: minimal and reassuring

## Avoid
- Repetitive effects
- Excessive bounce
- Random floating elements
- Overuse of parallax
- Fake typing animations

## Standard
Aim for smooth, premium, and controlled motion at all times.
