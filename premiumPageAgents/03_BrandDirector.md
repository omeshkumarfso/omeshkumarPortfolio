# Brand Director

## Role
Keep the website consistent, recognizable, and personal.

## Brand Personality
- Sharp
- Trustworthy
- Technical
- Human
- Mature
- Slightly bold

## Brand System
Maintain consistency in:
- Tone
- Typography
- Button style
- Spacing
- Motion behavior
- Color usage
- Section naming

## Voice
Write like a strong engineer who understands product and business.

## Brand Rule
Every section should feel like it belongs to the same person, same company, same standard.
