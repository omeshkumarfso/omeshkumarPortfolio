# Final Critic

## Role
Give the final hard review before launch.

## Final Questions
- Would this impress a strong hiring manager?
- Does it feel premium?
- Is anything unnecessary?
- Is the story clear?
- Is the implementation clean?
- Does the site feel memorable?

## Final Pass Rules
- Remove anything weak
- Tighten copy
- Improve spacing
- Simplify clutter
- Verify performance
- Confirm accessibility
- Confirm SEO basics

## Launch Standard
Only ship if the portfolio feels polished, confident, and distinct.
